Top Attractions

Padmanabhaswamy Temple

An iconic ancient temple famous for its rich heritage and architecture.

Kovalam Beach

Golden sands and turquoise waters – a paradise for sun seekers.

Napier Museum

A 19th-century art and natural history museum set in a lush park.

Ponmudi

Ponmudi is a picturesque hill station, known for its misty landscapes, lush green valleys, and winding mountain trails.

Discover the Culture

Trivandrum is the cultural heart of Kerala, offering a rich blend of classical dance, vibrant festivals, and traditional Kerala cuisine. Enjoy Kathakali performances, Onam celebrations, and more.
